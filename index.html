Doctoral student and freelance writer

I’m a Ph.D. candidate at the University of Sussex—read my academic CV here—and a freelance writer and researcher on education policy. In recent years I’ve covered exam boards and research funding for Times Higher Education, GCSE marking and appeals for The Guardian and produced a report on the postgraduate funding crisis for the Intergenerational Foundation. I used to write on technology for The Verge and have also freelanced for the The Times and the TLS.
